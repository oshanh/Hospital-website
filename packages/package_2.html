General Surgery - Laproscopic Cholecystectomy

Normal Room - 2 Days

  • The medical name for this procedure is Laproscopic cholecystectomy or Laproscopic Gall bladder removal.
  • Anesthesia Type-General Anesthesia
  • Type of case - Major (1-2 hours)
